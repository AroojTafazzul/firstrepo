From this screen, you will be able to submit a request for a Initial Drawdown provided by LoanIQ.

The menu on the left hand side of the screen will also let you to request for an Increase in existing Loan, or gain access to various reporting or Inquiry functions.

The last menu items let you to maintain existing set of loans, especially in case of Purged and Expired transactions.

In all pages containing an entry form, you shall have an access to the help pages through an icon dedicated to the respective function.
