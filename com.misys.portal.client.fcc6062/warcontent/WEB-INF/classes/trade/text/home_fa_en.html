From this screen, you will be able to initiate your Advance Payment Request and submit them to the Bank of your choice, as well as update them later on by issuing free format messages to the bank handling the file.

You can also access the list of all the currently Pending Transactions, including the Factoring that have been submitted to the bank but not yet acknowledged by it.

The existing transactions can be inquired through the dedicated Inquiry function. The list of transactions that are not submitted to the bank and that the user may want to purge as well as old transaction records purged by the issuing bank can be removed from the system with the FA Maintenance menu item.
