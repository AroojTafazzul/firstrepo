From this screen, you will be able to initiate your Invoices and present them to the Bank of your choice.

The menu of the left hand side of the screen will also let you amend an existing invoice (IN), or gain access to various reporting or inquiry functions.

The last menu item will let you maintain your existing set of Invoices, especially in case you want to purge old and expired transactions.

In all page containing an entry form, you will have access to the help pages through an icon dedicated to this function.
