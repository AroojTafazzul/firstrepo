From this screen, you will be able to initiate your TMA Messages ... and submit them to the Issuing Bank of your choice, as well as amend them later on or issue free format messages during the course of their life cycle to notify the bank of any event pertaining to them.

The menu of the left hand side of the screen will also let you amend an existing purchase order (PO), or gain access to various reporting or inquiry functions.

The last menu item will let you maintain your existing set of Purchase Orders, especially in case you want to purge old and expired transactions.

In all page containing an entry form, you will have access to the help pages through an icon dedicated to this function.
