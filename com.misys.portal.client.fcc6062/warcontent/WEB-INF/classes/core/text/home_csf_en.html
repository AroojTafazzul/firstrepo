From this screen, the user and most likely the one playing the role of the administrator in the company is able to access the System Features. Depending on its jurisdiction and profile, the user can maintain various characteristics of the system.

The following items can be reviewed or altered :

  • The password of the current user,
  • Users of the company and their permissions are also maintained from this screen,
  • The screen also provides a way to maintain static data such as the counterparties, accounts and phrases available in the system in order to let the user speed up its data input in the transaction screens.
