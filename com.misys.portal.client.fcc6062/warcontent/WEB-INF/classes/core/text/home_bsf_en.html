From this screen, the bank officer is able to access the System Features. Depending on its jurisdiction and profile, the user can maintain various characteristics of the system.

The following items can be reviewed or altered :

  • The password of the current user,
  • The way the jurisdiction is organised throughout the portal,
  • Customer companies can be created together with their initial administrator as well as its key characteristics,
  • Bank users and their permissions are also maintained from this screen,
