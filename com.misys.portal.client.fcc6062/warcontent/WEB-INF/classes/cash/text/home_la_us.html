From this screen, you will be able to initiate your Loan contracts and submit them to the Issuing Bank of your choice.

The menu of the left hand side of the screen will also let you access to various reporting or inquiry functions.

The last menu item will let you maintain your existing set of Foreign Exchange contracts, especially in case you want to purge old and expired transactions.

In all pages containing an entry form, you will have access to the help pages through an icon dedicated to this function.
